How Google Tracks Your Every Move

Google has built an invisible surveillance empire. Every product you use feeds them more data:

  • Location Tracking: Even with "Location History" off, your GPS, Wi-Fi, and even photos are logged.
  • Search & Browsing: Every query, click, and website visit — stored forever to profile your mind.
  • Android Phones: Your device constantly whispers: app usage, battery status, idle data, background mic activation.
  • YouTube: Every video you watch builds a psychological profile of your interests, habits, and moods.
  • Gmail: Scans your messages and even drafts to build ad-targeting dossiers.
  • Voice Assistants: “OK Google” listens for commands — but researchers found snippets of private conversations stored.
  • Ads & Cookies: Hidden trackers follow you across millions of websites — even outside Google platforms.

You are not the customer. You are the product. Every step, every click, every whisper — monetized.

Google’s Darkest Lawsuits

  • Incognito Mode Case (2020): Google sued for secretly tracking users in private browsing.
  • Location Tracking Lawsuit (2022): U.S. states accused Google of lying about stopping location tracking.
  • Antitrust Case (2023): U.S. Justice Dept. sued Google for monopolizing search & ads market.
  • EU Fines (2017-2019): Over €8 billion in fines for abusing dominance & violating user privacy.
  • Children’s Data Violation (YouTube, 2019): Google fined for illegally collecting kids’ personal data.

Even billion-dollar penalties couldn’t stop the hunger for your data.
