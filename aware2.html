Unveiling Google’s Black Deeds

Location Tracking

Even when users turned off location history, Google secretly continued to track and store location data. This was revealed in lawsuits, proving that privacy settings were misleading.

Incognito Mode Myth

In 2020, it was exposed that Google still collected browsing activity in Incognito Mode. Users believed they were private, but their data was still being harvested.

Gmail & Data Mining

Google scans Gmail accounts to build ad profiles. Even deleted emails and drafts have been reported to remain in Google’s hidden storage systems.

Android Surveillance

Android phones constantly send data back to Google — including app usage, system activity, and device location — even when not in use.
